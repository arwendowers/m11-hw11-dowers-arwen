Arwen Dowers

I'm a graduate student at UF studying Web Design. My primary career goal is to become a UX Designer. My interests include music, the ocean, and Pokémon. My hobbies include Pokémon Go, singing and playing piano, and scuba diving.

I have 3 cats: Stormy, Twix, and Phantom (from left to right). Stormy is a loving male lap cat. Twix is a sweet and soft long-haired female tortoiseshell. Phantom is a chaotic female kitten that loves to be held.

I got scuba certified in 2020 and 2021, up to PADI Rescue Diver status. I love seeing all the cool fish and coral, and I've dove in Roatan, Honduras and the Florida Keys. The coolest things I've seen include a nurse shark, stingrays, and an octopus.

I'm very involved with the UF Pokémon Go Players club, serving as a Niantic Community Ambassador and their Social Media Manager. I love hanging out with my friends while catching shiny Pokémon and battling in raids together.

EDM music is my favorite music genre and one of my biggest passions. My favorite artists are Rezz and Deadmau5. I love attending EDM music festivals, and I attend Rezz Rocks at Red Rocks in Denver every fall.
